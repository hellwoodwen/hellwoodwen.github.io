Studies

Wen Wen is a young artist who delves into kinect and performance art. Her works are inspired by psychedelic culture, social equality, and feminism.

After obtaining a bachelor's degree in the Japanese language at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, she is now studying Design for Performance and Interaction at The Bartlett School of Architecture, University College London.
