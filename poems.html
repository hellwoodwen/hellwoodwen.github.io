Daily

Venus without arms
Looked down upon by the children of goldsmith
Laying on the abyss/hiatus
A plume of silk
Sitting afloat as a saviour
Dark sarcasm and sweet love
Spinning along mobius strip
Overflowing imageries turn into disgust
Youth start with the folly of admirations
End with thorough understandings

Koran

Children with the third eye are lucid dreaming
Planes are assembled while they are in flight
Always easier to write on back than on the front
Life has loops and you can break
From the back
Wick it or wreck it
Jump up after a while as zombie revival
Or flying dutchman
Only because the world is mad
Doesn’t mean you have to be the same
The world is not going to change
And so do I
Me - an earthly grown northern American califlower
Silent killer
Cocaine powder
Abel grained flour

people think I’m sliding

I might look like sliding
Or people think that way
In fact I am surfing
it’s freer without trail
the tides go up and down
I can predict the direction
Though not in detail
for macro and micro worlds have not yet united
I doubt if they need to be
it is good to have servers of different functions in networks
noblesse, pride, and supremacy ought to be buried with feudalism, imperialism, and monarchy
There are rotten people living in the world with crowns
zombies a.k.a mummies from beneath the pyramids
disguise as persons of importance
when all their actions are a mere decoration
they are criminals producing fantasies for those who are looking back at the old days
one leg in the tomb and the other struggling to survive
live in constant fluxes
and you won’t have any problems of life and death, nor money, nor love

Pre-History No Memory

The Zerg swarms and the protoss teleports
Attempting for balance in the midland of terrans
The shadow underlines a unpenetrable dark core
Energy waves are tangent to its edge
Thick and calm inside
Pipping hot
A scene only visible after the explosion

The long bursts of Gamma-rays contain information
Butterflies break from cocoons
Clouds carry on slowly in front of a blue sky background
The wind in autumn is red, green and blue
Passes through the retina to form elusive floaters
Fear
Is useless
Emotions
Are invented
Like a sharpened pencil
It could only break
There is no going back
